PENDULUM

The Bio

The boys from pendulum (the boiz, from here on in) were forged from the creative fire that is Perth, and after creating numerous bangers, were invited to tour the up over lands. The world grew understandably infatuated with the boiz, and offered them much gold to produce their rythmic ether.

And life was good, hits were made, good times were had and the good feels were about for the boiz
